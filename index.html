Things at School

UCLA VAST Lab Undergraduate Researcher

My project was about qubit mapping in reconfigurable atom arrays during quantum circuit compilation. My main tasks included tweaking SMT solver constraints in the codebase to standardize the output generated by greedy and optimal quantum compiling algorithms and streamlining quantum compilation workflow by integrating the optimal and greedy compilers.

IEEE Pocket Racers

My second yearlong (and ongoing) project with IEEE. This project focuses on computer vision and machine learning. We're trying to build a car that can drive around a racetrack from images it "sees" from a camera! So far, we've been learning about some math behind ML and will begin to work on the actual car in spring quarter

Corkboard

Corkboard is my CS 35L project, where my team built a webapp to help roommates track and manage their fridge space. I mainly worked on the backend side of things, including storing, updating, and deleting data from our firebase database, as well as the frontend logic to manage react states and display data queries from the database. It was my first real experience working in backend, so it was super rewarding! 100% would do again.
